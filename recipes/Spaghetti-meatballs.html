Spaghetti with meatballs

Description

Mildly awesome recipe for spaghetti and meatballs with ketchup

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Meatballs
  • ketchup

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 to 12 minutes; rinse quickly under cold water and drain.
  2. Cook meatballs in a pan with some butter at medium to high heat
  3. Serve with ketchup and a cold glass of milk
